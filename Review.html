Dream Cake is a specialty bakery that puts together the right mix of sweetness and creativity. Designing custom cakes and baking tiny d inelectables from cupcakes, cake pops, macarons to wedding cakes and birthday cakes. Our decorators and designers take cakery to a new level. Dream Cake Bakery started as a home-based business in 2006, owned and operated by Roshel De Villa. It all began, when Roshel went on maternity leave in 2007 with her second child. With her passion in baking, she created cakes for her son every month until he turned ONE. Many of her friends saw her cake creations and loved them. Roshel created a Facebook page in 2008 and though social media and word of mouth she gained clientels, more of them were repeat business. In the beginning of October 2014 Dream Cakes Bakery opened its retail shop in Coquitlam B.C.
